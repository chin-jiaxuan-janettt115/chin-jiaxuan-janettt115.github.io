Lee Hom Wang

Leehom Wang, born on May 17, 1976, in Rochester, New York, is a highly acclaimed Taiwanese-American singer, songwriter, actor, and producer. Known for his exceptional musical talent, he skillfully blends pop, R&B, hip-hop, and traditional Chinese elements in his songs. Often regarded as one of the most influential figures in Mandopop, Wang has inspired millions through his creativity and dedication to music.

Beyond his singing career, he has also acted in several films and composed soundtracks that showcase his musical versatility. His passion for promoting Chinese culture through modern music has earned him worldwide recognition and lasting admiration.
